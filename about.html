About Us

Welcome to EXCELLENT LADIES OF VALOUR AND STEEL FOUNDATION INTERNATIONAL

The noble NGO Elovas was established on November 20, 2011. We have members all around the nation as well as abroad.

Women's human rights, gender equality, and the empowerment of women and girls are the primary focus of the female-led, non-profit organization known as EXCELLENT LADIES OF VALOUR AND STEEL FOUNDATION INTERNATIONAL (Elovas). In Nigeria, there are significant gender differences in terms of political engagement, economic empowerment, living standards, and access to technology. We believe that women empowerment and gender equality are essential to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals and that doing so will improve Nigeria (SDG).

We can sum up our efforts by saying that we impact lives positively.

Worldwide partners

Founded in 2011

The Excellent Ladies of Valour and Steel Foundation (Elovas), a non-profit organization, was founded on November 20, 2011. We have members living both domestically and internationally.

Our Mission

Elovas works nonstop to save lives, end poverty, and bring about social justice.

Our Vision

We seek a world of hope, inclusion, and social justice, where poverty has been overcome and all people live with dignity and security.
